hotaru(n) is a Discord bot that does ... funny things. He is written with Javascript (Node.js) and uses Discord.js to interact with the Discord API.

He is created and maintained by yours truly (also @kyu_rem_ on Discord). He started as an after-school pastime they made out of curiosity. Years later, lots of things happened, and he's like this now. At least he's funny...?

Not much is known about his past. Legends say he had a much different name and personality back then, but both he and his creator don't talk about this fact at all. Maybe it's best to leave it alone.
